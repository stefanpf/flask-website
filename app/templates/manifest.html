Manifesto and Privacy Policy

I value simplicity and privacy.

Simplicity: I want to keep things simple on this website. Is it as simple as possible? No. The site is a learning experience for me and I do use technology that might not be strictly necessary. But: I always keep you, the visitor, in mind. I want this site to be lightweight and easy to read. I do not use a pre-made solution that might include features I don't need. I implement only the things I deem useful. Simply put: no ads, no bloat, no distractions.

Privacy: I value my privacy and I value yours. This means that I do not use cookies on this website. Nor do I use any tracking tools such as Google Analytics. I don't need to know how many people visit this website and where they are from. The company I host this site with does keep server logs which include the IP addresses of visitors. These logs are kept for debugging purposes and are automatically deleted after some time has passed. I do not access these logs. Simply put: I don't want anyone to keep my data and I won't keep yours.

If you have any questions about this I would love to hear from you at mail@stefanpfeuffer.com.
